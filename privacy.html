Privacy Policy

Your workflow stays yours.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Orvesca is a Windows desktop workflow tool. This policy explains what information Orvesca handles, where it is kept, and who it is shared with. In short: Orvesca has no user account, does not track you, and keeps your workflow data on your own computer.

Who we are

"Orvesca", "we", "us" and "our" refer to the makers of the Orvesca application and website (orvesca.github.io). You can reach us any time at orvescaapp@gmail.com.

Information handled when you buy a plan

Purchases are processed by our payment provider, Lemon Squeezy, which acts as the Merchant of Record for every sale. When you buy the Annual Pass or Lifetime Pro, you enter your email and payment details on Lemon Squeezy's secure checkout — not inside Orvesca. Lemon Squeezy collects and processes that purchase and payment information under its own privacy policy.

We do not run our own servers that store your name, email, or payment details. We receive only what Lemon Squeezy provides to fulfil your order (such as your order details and license key).

Information stored on your device

Orvesca stores a small amount of data locally on your own computer, in files that never leave your device except for the license check described below:

License file

To keep your paid plan active, Orvesca saves a local license file containing your license key, license type, subscription status, the customer name associated with the purchase, a device identifier generated for your installation, and timestamps for activation and the most recent online check.

Workflow file

The workflow you build — the order of your windows, your chosen jump keys, whether jumping is on, your notes, and whether each note is shown — is saved in a local file kept alongside the license file. This workflow data stays on your device and is never sent to us.

Windows Orvesca can see

To let you build a workflow, Orvesca reads the list of open windows shown in your Windows taskbar (for example, their titles) so you can add them to your path, reorder them, and jump between them. Orvesca does not read, capture, record, or transmit the contents of those windows — only their presence in the taskbar, and only on your device in real time. This information is not sent to us or to anyone else.

License verification

For paid plans, Orvesca contacts Lemon Squeezy's licensing service once a week to confirm your license is still active and to allow use across the devices you manage. If the device is offline, the check is retried and your plan keeps working for a grace period of up to three weeks in total; you can also trigger a check yourself with the Sync button.

Each check sends your license key and your installation's device identifier, and receives back your activation status and the customer name shown in the app. No workflow data, notes, window titles, or window contents are included in this check.

What we do not do

  • We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising.
  • We do not build user profiles or sell, rent, or trade any personal information.
  • We do not require you to create an account to use Orvesca.

Retention and control

Because the license and workflow files live on your computer, you remain in control of them. Uninstalling Orvesca or deleting those files removes that local data from your device. For purchase-related information held by Lemon Squeezy, you can exercise your rights through Lemon Squeezy's customer portal or by contacting us and we will help direct your request.

Third-party services

Orvesca relies on Lemon Squeezy for payments and license management. Their handling of your data is governed by Lemon Squeezy's own privacy policy, available on their website.

Children

Orvesca is a productivity tool intended for a general, working audience and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as Orvesca evolves. When we do, we will change the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Orvesca after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email orvescaapp@gmail.com and we will respond as soon as we can.
