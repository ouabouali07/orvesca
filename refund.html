Refund Policy

Try it fully, then decide.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

We want you to be sure about Orvesca before you pay for it — so we make that easy. Orvesca offers a Free plan with no time limit and a 14-day trial that unlocks every feature, with no card required. You can explore the full experience, on your own windows and workflows, before spending anything.

All sales are final

Because you can evaluate Orvesca completely before buying, all purchases of the Annual Pass and Lifetime Pro are final, and we do not offer refunds for a change of mind or for features you decided you did not need after purchase.

Buyer protection through Lemon Squeezy. Our payments are handled by Lemon Squeezy as the Merchant of Record. Lemon Squeezy may, at its own discretion and under its own policies and applicable law, issue a refund or resolve a payment dispute. Any statutory consumer rights you have under the law of your country of residence are not affected by this policy.

If something goes wrong

A final-sale policy is not a no-help policy. If Orvesca will not activate, your license key does not arrive, you were charged incorrectly, or you hit a genuine technical problem, contact us and we will work with you to fix it. In cases of duplicate charges or clear billing errors, we will help arrange a correction.

Cancelling the Annual Pass

The Annual Pass renews automatically each year. You can cancel future renewals at any time through the Lemon Squeezy customer portal (the link is in your order confirmation email) or by contacting us. Cancelling stops the next renewal; your access continues until the end of the period you have already paid for. The Lifetime Pro plan is a one-time purchase and does not renew.

If you upgrade to Lifetime Pro

Buying Lifetime Pro while you hold an Annual Pass is a separate purchase. It does not cancel your annual subscription — you need to cancel that yourself in the Lemon Squeezy customer portal, or it will renew and charge you again. Payments already made on the Annual Pass are not credited or refunded against the Lifetime Pro price. Orvesca asks you to confirm both points in the app before the purchase.

How to reach us

For any billing or refund question, email orvescaapp@gmail.com. Please include your order details or license key so we can help you quickly.
